Our Story

Hi, my name is Evan, and i love beer! Due to worldwide events in 2020 you may be familiar with, beer was in short supply throughout much of the UK. I could've simply stocked up on crates and crates of beer, or have gone without for months on end. Neither was an option I was willing to accept.

Lockdown was pretty lonely, and the days long. Work was pretty tough going and I needed to fill my evenings with activities to stay sane. My friends were in a similar situation, and after many boozy zoom calls we came up with the idea of brewing our own beer to beat the drinks drought.

We decided to set ourselves a lockdown project around brewing beer. We would each learn how to brew beers, try different recipes and post them to eachother to sample and review on a weekly basis. Of course the first few batches were undrinkable, but we kept at it until we felt proud of our beers. Lockdown may have ended, but we decided to keep that same energy going.

We became confident enough with our own brewing skills that we were able to quit our day jobs, and sell a small range of our individual homemade beer recipes. Although we're still a small startup, our ambitions were set high, and we have progressed to the point where our beers are stocked in small craft beer shops, we have been able to curate a small selection of international beers. Excitingly, we have expanded to include a range of brewing starter kits and recipe boxes, featuring a beer of the month, hops and special recipes, a magazine, and how to guides. With the already expensive pre-pandemic average drink price soaring to over £6, now has never been a better time to just do it yourself.

Love from - "The HomeBrew Crew"

HomeBrew is run by Evan Parker, Neel Patel, Jay Mistry and Luke Prescott. We wanted our company to be a hub for beer lovers, those whose passion goes beyond big corporate beer makers and towards something more grass roots. We truly believe that anyone is capable of brewing their own beer, and want to cultivate a new generation of beer brewers
