About us

HomeBrew began as a covid-19 pandemic lockdown project between 4 likeminded friends. No open? No problem! We created HomeBrew after we realised how easy it is to brew beer, and wanted to get people brewing their own. We offer a range of starter kits and monthly recipe boxes, alongside our own personal beers, and a small range of international beers.
